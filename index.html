I am a National Science Foundation (NSF) Postdoctoral Fellow at Universit√§t Bonn, working with Markus Hausmann. Previously, I was a postdoc at Universiteit Utrecht with Lennart Meier and a graduate student at UCLA, where my advisor was Mike Hill. My CV is here.

My research is in equivariant, chromatic, synthetic, and motivic homotopy. I am particularly interested in (connective) higher real $K$-theories and the various computations one can perform with these theories (e.g. their slice, homotopy fixed points, Adams, Adams-Novikov, etc. spectral sequences).
